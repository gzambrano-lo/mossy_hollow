Who We Are

Mossy Hollow was created from a love of tiny worlds, soft creatures and cozy small objects that make everyday feel magical.

We take inspiration from collectible brands like Smiski, and Sumikko Gurashi, along with old stationery shops, the mossy forest environment, and the charm of small things.
